## What Makes Highwood Poly-Lumber Actually Maintenance-Free

Highwood's HDPE poly-lumber is manufactured from post-consumer recycled plastic — primarily milk jugs and detergent bottles — processed into a purified feedstock and extruded into structural boards. The material is non-porous, which means it doesn't absorb moisture. No moisture absorption means no rot, no cracking from freeze-thaw cycles, and no support for mold or mildew growth.

Color is compounded into the HDPE during extrusion, not applied as a surface coat. A scratch reveals identical color underneath. UV stabilizers are built into the material the same way — compounded in during manufacturing rather than applied on top. These two properties together eliminate the primary failure modes of painted or stained wood: coating adhesion failure and surface UV degradation.

All Lehigh pieces use 304-grade stainless steel hardware throughout. In coastal environments where standard steel fasteners oxidize within a season or two of salt-air exposure, the stainless specification matters for long-term structural integrity. Highwood backs the full collection with a 20-year limited residential warranty against rotting, splintering, and structural failure. Maintenance is a wash with mild soap and water, seasonally or as needed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the most durable material for an outdoor garden bench?**
HDPE poly-lumber is the strongest case for zero-maintenance durability. It's non-porous, UV-stabilized through the full material thickness, and freeze-thaw stable. The protection is built into the material rather than applied as a surface coating, which eliminates the primary long-term failure mode of painted or treated wood alternatives.

**Can poly-lumber benches be left out in winter?**
Yes. HDPE doesn't absorb water, so freeze-thaw cycles have no structural effect. Wood traps moisture in its cellular structure and can crack when that moisture freezes and expands. Poly-lumber has no such failure mode and requires no winter storage or protective covering.

**How much weight can an outdoor garden bench hold?**
The Highwood Lehigh bench and gliding bench are both rated at 500 lbs, which covers two average-sized adults with significant margin. Porch swing load capacity depends on the overhead anchor point as well as the swing itself — verify the supporting structure's dynamic load rating before installation.
