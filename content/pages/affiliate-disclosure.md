# Affiliate Disclosure

*Last updated: March 2025*

## FTC Compliance Statement

HomesAndGardenDecor.com is a participant in affiliate advertising programs designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to third-party retailers and manufacturers. When you click a product link on this site and make a purchase, we may receive a commission from the retailer at no additional cost to you.

## Our Affiliate Programs

We currently participate in the following affiliate programs:

- **Rakuten Advertising** — linking to Waterdrop, Highwood USA, Easy Plumbing, and other home improvement brands
- Additional affiliate programs may be added as the site grows

## How This Affects Our Reviews

It doesn't. Our editorial policy requires every product review to include a genuine limitations section. We don't publish reviews that are pure endorsements. If a product has real tradeoffs, we document them. Affiliate commission rates do not factor into our product selection or editorial ratings.

## Identifying Affiliate Links

All affiliate links on this site are identified with an "affiliate link" notation. We do not attempt to obscure the nature of affiliate relationships.
