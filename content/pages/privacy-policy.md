# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2025*

## Information We Collect

HomesAndGardenDecor.com collects limited information to operate and improve the site:

- **Usage data** — pages visited, time on site, referring pages, collected via Google Analytics (anonymized)
- **Contact information** — only if you email us directly

## Cookies

This site uses cookies for Google Analytics tracking and affiliate program attribution (Rakuten). These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information beyond standard web analytics data.

## Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site. Data is aggregated and anonymized. You can opt out via the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## Affiliate Links

When you click affiliate links, you are directed to third-party retailer websites operating under their own privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties.

## Contact

Privacy questions: [admin@irongatestrategic.com](mailto:admin@irongatestrategic.com)
