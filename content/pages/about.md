# About Homes &amp; Garden Decor

## What We Do

HomesAndGardenDecor.com publishes independent technical reviews of home improvement products — with a focus on water filtration systems, outdoor furniture, kitchen fixtures, and the infrastructure that makes a home function well over the long term.

Our review framework is borrowed from professional infrastructure evaluation: we lead with specifications, analyze total cost of ownership, and include a genuine "think carefully before purchasing" section in every review. We don't write pure product endorsements. We write honest assessments of what a product does well, where it has real limitations, and whether the TCO math makes sense for the intended use case.

---

## Who Writes Here

**Jeff M.** is the primary analyst and author. With 20+ years of experience evaluating mission-critical systems and a background in residential infrastructure, Jeff applies engineering-grade evaluation standards to home improvement products. He is a U.S. Navy veteran and writes from Mississippi.
